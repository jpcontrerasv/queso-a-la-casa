Reminder for WooCommerce Unpaid Orders
---------------------------------------

version 1.0 :
- New email "Payment reminder". To configure : WooCommerce > Settings > Emails
- New action button in orders panel to send the email
- New "email resend action" in the order detail to send the email 
- Compatible with Wordpress 3.8.1 and WooCommerce 2.0 or 2.1
